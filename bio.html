Short Biography

I am from Chengdu, China. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Statistics in 2011, and a Master's course in Probability and Statistics in 2013 from School of Management, Fudan University, before I come to UCSD. I obtained my PhD degree from UCSD mathematics in March 2018.

I worked for YouTube for three years, mainly on YouTube Premium related problems. Before that, I worked for Amazon Flex Science for one year, and for Microsoft Advertising for two years. At Microsoft I was in the pricing and ranking team in Native Ads (display ads) group.
